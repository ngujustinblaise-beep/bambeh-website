About Bambeh

The app that carries all loads

Bambeh is an all-in-one marketplace, delivery, and payments platform built for Cameroon and Africa. Our name comes from the local word meaning "baby" — because just like a baby carried on a mother's back, Bambeh carries all your needs.

Our Mission

To connect buyers, sellers, workers, and communities across Cameroon and beyond — making everyday commerce safe, simple, and accessible to everyone, regardless of location or income level.

What we offer

We bring together a marketplace for goods, a job board, housing and vehicle rentals, farm produce, group buying (tontines/njangi), secure mobile money payments, and an AI chatbot assistant — all under one roof.

Where we operate

We are currently operating in Cameroon, with headquarters in Yaoundé, and expanding rapidly across Central and West Africa. Our platform supports English, French, Hausa, and Arabic.

Our team

Bambeh is built by a passionate team of Cameroonian entrepreneurs and technologists committed to transforming African commerce. We believe in local solutions built for local realities.

Privacy Policy
